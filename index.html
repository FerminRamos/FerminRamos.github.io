About Me

A data analyst and computer scientist with a proven track record of transforming complex data into actionable insights. I’ve published academic research on health and socio-economic disparities, contributed to federally-affiliated analytical work through a highly selective data program with the CIA, and supported research teams in identifying community vulnerabilities using SQL, Python, Tableau, and ArcGIS. I’ve also won two competitive hackathons that led to investor-backed startup ventures. Now, I’m excited to apply my analytical mindset, risk awareness, and experience with large-scale data systems to the financial sector - especially in roles focused on forecasting, fraud detection, or investment analytics.
